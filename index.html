Who We Are

Pegorion Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking product-based software company that creates solutions to meet the needs of modern businesses. We focus on transforming complex problems into simple, user-friendly applications that provide significant value to our users.

We are committed to advancing from traditional industry practices to advanced, technology-driven solutions. Our flagship product, qrder, demonstrates our dedication to enhancing the restaurant and hospitality industry.
